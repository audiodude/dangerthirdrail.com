Radio Machine is the first EP from Danger Third Rail. It features five songs which increase in intensity and tempo from a somewhat mellow ballad (the title track) to a frenzied punk song ("Scream Into The Void").

Released in September 2018, Radio Machine is Danger Third Rail's most ambitious project to date.

Bandcamp iTunes Spotify Google Play Amazon Music

Pink Fluffy Cloud is Danger Third Rail's debut single. Included are two songs, an A side (the title track, "Pink Fluffy Cloud") and a B side (the punk style number "No More"). The single features Dan Sherizan on bass guitar and session drums from Emily Dolan Davies.

Danger Third Rail is a recording project of Travis Briggs. You can email your feedback to travis@travisbriggs.com, whether you love the songs or hate them!

All songs written by Travis Briggs. All songs recorded primarily in Travis' kitchen/home recording studio in San Francisco, CA. All drums by Emily Dolan Davies. Album art for Pink Fluffy Cloud and Radio Machine done by artists for hire on Fiverr.

Travis would like to thank his beautiful wife Abby for always being supportive of his creative endeavors. He would also like to thank Emily Dolan Davies, without whom these recordings would not be possible. Finally, Travis would like to thank his family for all of their support of his music growing up.
