Radio Machine is the first EP from Danger Third Rail. It features five songs which increase in intensity and tempo from a somewhat mellow ballad (the title track) to a frenzied punk song ("Scream Into The Void").

Released in September 2018, Radio Machine is Danger Third Rail's most ambitious project to date.

Pink Fluffy Cloud is Danger Third Rail's debut single. Included are two songs, an A side (the title track, "Pink Fluffy Cloud") and a B side (the punk style number "No More"). The single features Dan Sherizan on bass guitar and session drums from Emily Dolan Davies.
